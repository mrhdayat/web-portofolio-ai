# Fiverr Portfolio Project: The Devetesion AI Campaign

## Project Name
**Devetesion: High-Action AI Technical-Wear Editorial**

## Project Description
**Client & Context**
This project was developed for Devetesion, an emerging label focused on high-performance technical wear. The requirement was a series of high-action editorial shots that captured the movement and durability of the apparel in harsh, industrial, and winter environments.

**The Goal**
To create a cinematic "lookbook" that combines the grit of industrial locations with the sharpness of high-fashion photography. The visual direction needed to be aggressive, technical, and hyper-realistic.

**Challenges**
The main difficulty lay in the complex interactions of light—specifically reflections on goggles and the behavior of multi-layered technical fabrics during dynamic movement (jumping/sprinting). AI often struggles with these fine-tuned physics; my goal was to ensure every strap and reflection felt authentic.

**The Solution**
I implemented a specialized AI workflow using high-resolution diffusion models and custom aesthetic embeddings. By leveraging advanced ControlNet parameters, I was able to direct the "models" into precise high-action poses while maintaining the structural integrity of the techwear gear. The final series delivers a professional campaign aesthetic that stands up to the scrutiny of high-fashion standards.

**Project Name**
Industrial Tech-Wear AI Brand Campaign

**Project Description**
The client required a high-fidelity brand campaign for a new line of technical streetwear. The goal was to emphasize the durability and industrial nature of the garments through atmospheric, gritty environmental photography and macro shots of fabric textures.

The primary challenge was achieving a specific "industrial grit" without losing the clean lines of the garment design. We needed to show the clothes in motion—running, walking, and interacting with harsh architectural spaces—while maintaining extreme realism in how light hit the synthetic fabrics and weaves.

I approached this by building a dedicated AI art direction framework focused on "Atmospheric Industrialism." I prioritized high-contrast, moody lighting and a desaturated color palette to make the textures pop. By leveraging advanced image-to-image and depth-aware generation techniques, I ensured that every shot felt like part of a unified, expensive brand ecosystem. The final delivery included a full suite of lookbook assets and macro product details.

# Fiverr Portfolio Project: The AI Editorial Masterclass

## Project Name
**Editorial Mastery: High-Fashion AI Studio Series**

## Project Description
**Client & Context**
The objective of this project was to push the limits of AI-generated editorial photography for a premium lifestyle label. The brand required a lookbook that focused on "Human Emotion" and "Extreme Angles," moving away from the standard posed look toward something more artistic and intimate.

**The Goal**
To deliver a campaign that feels like a multi-day studio session with top-tier models. The focus was on extreme low-angle shots and high-contrast editorial lighting to create a "Vogue-Standard" visual identity.

**Challenges**
Producing extreme angles (extreme low and high) often leads to anatomical distortion in standard AI generation. Additionally, maintaining realistic skin texture and light wrap on close-up facial shots required a significant amount of post-prompting refinement and negative-weight balancing.

**The Solution**
I utilized a custom virtual camera workflow, simulating 35mm and 85mm prime lenses to achieve the correct depth of field and perspective. By focusing on "Texture Preservation" rather than "Smoothness," the final results have the characteristic grain and imperfection of real film. This campaign provides the client with an elite visual library suitable for billboards and high-end digital marketing.

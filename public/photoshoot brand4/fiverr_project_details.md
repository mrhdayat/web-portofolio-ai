# Fiverr Portfolio Project: The Scene-Lock AI Series

## Project Name
**Avant-Garde AI Branding: The Scene-Lock Campaign**

## Project Description
**Client & Context**
This project was part of a series for the Devetesion label, specifically focusing on an "Avant-Garde" industrial aesthetic. The client required a cohesive lookbook where the background and lighting remained static while the model's interaction with the environment evolved.

**The Goal**
To demonstrate "Scene-Locking" capabilities—creating a unified brand world where multiple shots feel like they were captured in the same physical space and time, maintaining absolute environmental consistency.

**Challenges**
The primary challenge was preventing background "drift" between generations. Traditional AI generation often alters small details in the environment when posing a model. My goal was to lock the industrial backdrop precisely so the viewer’s focus remains entirely on the apparel.

**The Solution**
I utilized a multi-layered ControlNet workflow combined with IP-Adapter masking to isolate and preserve the background. By "pinning" the environmental details, I was able to iterate through various high-fashion poses and lighting shifts on the model without disturbing the scene's geometry. The resulting series functions as a professional-grade brand campaign with flawless continuity.

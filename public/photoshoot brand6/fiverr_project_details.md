# Fiverr Portfolio Project: The Triple-Lock Consistency Campaign

## Project Name
**Triple-Lock Consistency: AI Character & Product Lookbook**

## Project Description
**Client & Context**
The core objective of this project was to solve the "consistency gap" in AI branding. A boutique high-fashion label needed a lookbook where the same model wore the exact same technical outfit across multiple complex, geometric poses.

**The Goal**
To prove that AI can handle "Triple-Lock" requirements: absolute consistency of the Character (face), the Product (outfit details), and the Scene (lighting/vibe) simultaneously.

**Challenges**
Standard AI workflows often fail when models are placed in twisted or asymmetrical poses; the clothing details typically distort or the facial likeness drifts. For a real brand, this is unacceptable. Every buckle, strap, and seam on the technical gear had to remain identical across every frame.

**The Solution**
I implemented a high-level technical pipeline using LoRA (Large-scale Low-Rank Adaptation) for the specific garment and advanced face-consistency nodes. By "locking" these three pillars, I generated a series of avant-garde poses that maintain 100% brand integrity. This methodology allows clients to scale their marketing collateral indefinitely while ensuring their product and model stay perfectly on-brand.

**Project Name**
High-End AI Fashion Campaign Art Direction

**Project Description**
The client, a burgeoning fashion label, needed a visually arresting editorial campaign that captured an avant-garde, Y2K-inspired aesthetic. Their goal was to produce a high-fashion lookbook that felt both nostalgic and futuristic, with a focus on bold colors and striking group compositions.

The primary challenge was maintaining consistent brand identity across a diverse range of characters and outfits while using purely AI-generated imagery. Ensuring that the neons, leather textures, and complex tailoring felt "real" and expensive was a technical hurdle that required precise prompt engineering and post-production refinement.

I solved this by developing a multi-layered art direction strategy. I focused on a "cyber-industrial" lighting setup and a rigid color palette to unify the assets. By using advanced control networks for garment preservation and facial consistency, I delivered a 20+ image campaign that rivaled professional studio photography at a fraction of the traditional cost and turnaround time.

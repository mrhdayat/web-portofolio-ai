**Project Name**
Premium Skincare Art Direction & Digital Ad Campaign

**Project Description**
The client needed a digital advertising campaign for a high-end skincare line. The primary goal was to drive e-commerce conversions while strictly maintaining the brand's premium, minimalist identity across multiple formats (Instagram carousels, stories, and web banners).

The main challenge was balancing direct-response marketing (clear CTAs and hooks) with luxury aesthetics. Often, heavy ad copy clutters the visual space, making a premium product look cheap.

To solve this, I relied on strict whitespace management and a structured bento-grid layout system. I paired a classic serif font for authoritative headlines with a clean sans-serif for secondary text to ensure readability. I also applied consistent color grading across all raw photos to unify the lighting. The result is a highly legible, conversion-focused ad set that still feels like a high-end editorial piece.
